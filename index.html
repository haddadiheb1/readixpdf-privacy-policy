Permissions

To provide our specific features, we require access to certain permissions on your device:

1. Storage Permissions (Read/Write/Manage External Storage)

We use these permissions to allow you to list, read, manage, organize, and edit PDF files stored on your device. We prioritize your privacy: all file operations, including searching and editing, are performed entirely locally on your device. We do not upload your documents to any external server.

2. Camera Permission

We use the camera permission to enable the Document Scanner feature. This allows you to capture images of physical documents to convert them into PDF files. All image processing and OCR (Optical Character Recognition) is performed locally on your device.
